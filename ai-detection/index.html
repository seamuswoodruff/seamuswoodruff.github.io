The study

Two questions. One surprising answer.

Our team set out to answer two questions: does the device you view images on affect your ability to spot AI-generated ones? And does familiarity with AI technology make you better at detecting it?

Everything here is built from the same data. The quiz uses the same 80-image pool participants saw. The dataset is our final cleaned dataset prior to narrowing it down to our analytical sample, with some demographic information withheld for privacy purposes. The findings page is what we concluded, and what surprised us.
