The study

We designed this study around two research questions: whether the device you use to view an image affects your ability to detect AI, and whether knowing more about AI makes you better at spotting it. We asked 508 Bowdoin students, faculty, and staff to find out.

Laptop users outscored mobile users. Age predicted performance. AI familiarity only showed statistically significant results among faculty and staff. This website lets you take a version of the same ten-image test our participants saw, dig into the survey design, and explore our results in detail.
